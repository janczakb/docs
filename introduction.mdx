Filament Short URL is a drop-in plugin for Laravel 11+ and Filament v5 that transforms your existing application into a fully self-hosted link management platform. Instead of paying monthly fees to Bitly, Dub.co, or Rebrandly, you install one Composer package and own every part of the stack — your server, your database, your data, and zero artificial limits on links or clicks.

## Why Self-Hosted?

Running Filament Short URL on your own server means your click data never leaves your infrastructure. There are no per-seat fees, no click-count quotas, and no vendor lock-in. Features that cost hundreds of dollars per month on commercial platforms — custom domains, retargeting pixels, mobile deep linking, advanced analytics — are all included out of the box.

What makes this plugin unique compared to commercial alternatives is the combination of features that no single SaaS platform offers together: offline GDPR-safe Geo-IP via MaxMind, server-side GA4 analytics that bypass ad-blockers, VPN and bot filtering for clean stats, Google Safe Browsing checks on every saved URL, and full Filament panel integration with keyboard shortcuts, live activity feeds, and a QR designer — all in one package with a `$0` monthly cost.

## How It Integrates

The plugin registers itself automatically through Laravel's package discovery. Once installed, you add `FilamentShortUrlPlugin::make()` to your Filament panel provider and it appears as a fully functional section of your existing admin panel — no separate application to deploy, no separate database to manage.

<Note>
  Filament Short URL requires an active Filament v5 panel. If you haven't set up Filament yet, follow the [official Filament installation guide](https://filamentphp.com/docs/panels/installation) first, then return here.
</Note>
